Protecting Industrial Worker Health

Addressing the occupational hazards of air pollution

Workplace Air Quality: A Critical Health & Safety Issue

Industrial workers face significantly higher exposure to harmful pollutants, leading to increased risks of respiratory diseases, cardiovascular problems, and reduced productivity.

  • Construction workers exposed to PM2.5 have 30% higher risk of lung disease
  • Manufacturing employees show 25% more sick days due to pollution-related illnesses
  • Outdoor workers in polluted areas have decreased cognitive function
  • Long-term exposure increases risk of chronic conditions like asthma and COPD

Zylm Energy's industrial air purification solutions are specifically designed to protect worker health in challenging environments, reducing absenteeism and improving overall workplace safety.
