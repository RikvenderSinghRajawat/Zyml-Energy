AirBin - Outdoor

Zylm Energy has designed a way to protect the environment & living being from the atrocities of dirty air. AirBin is an air pollution control device which has automated control system and uses IOT and AI.

AirBin helps reduce infection and mortality through the use of effective and data-driven artificial intelligence control systems. AirBin is ready to operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week on an Autopilot mode.

Tech:

The unit has an IOT & AI for live monitoring. Designed to deliver clean air. AirBin is a next-generation device which can automate monitoring of air quality.

Sensors:

Nitrogen Oxide, Ozone, PM2.5, PM10, Carbon Monoxide, Sulphur Oxide, temperature, Humidity.

Controls:

Carbon Monoxide, PM 1 PM 2.5, PM 10, Sulphur Dioxide, Ozone, Carbon Monoxide, Nitrous Oxide, Dust particles, Heavy Metals, etc.

AirBin - Outdoor (basically usage +)

Utility

AirBin - Outdoor is a combination of 9 proven technology and methods used as an treatment control units installed in the city. The unit is designed to reduce air pollution & the waste is collected and disposed.
