AirBin - Outdoor

Zylm Energy has designed a way to protect the environment & living beings from the atrocities of dirty and polluted air with the brand - AirBin, which is a next-generation, multi-function for Outdoor / Indoor / RECD for DG set / Automobile / Pole.

AirBin is an air pollution control device which has automated control system and uses IOT and AI. AirBin helps reduce infection and mortality through the use of effective and data-driven artificial intelligence. Air quality and other environmental factors are continuously monitored and accurate data is identified using artificial intelligence.

Technology

The unit has an IoT & AI for live monitoring. Designed to deliver clean air, AirBin is a next-generation device which can automate monitoring of air quality. The Environmental Sensor Kit allows continuous monitoring of air quality.

Sensors

Nitrogen Oxide, Ozone, PM2.5, PM10, Carbon Monoxide, Sulphur Oxide, temperature, Humidity, Rain, etc.

Controls

Carbon Monoxide, PM 1, PM 2.5, PM 10, Sulphur Dioxide, Ozone, Carbon Monoxide, Nitrogen Oxide, Lead, Aerosol, Hydrocarbon, Synthetic Vitreous Fibre, Fuel Oils / Kerosene, TVOC, Poisonous Gases, Airborne Bacteria & Virus, Dust particles, Heavy Metals, etc.

Utility

AirBin - Outdoor is a combination of 9 proven technology and methods used for air treatment combined in a unit for purification of polluted air. Live monitoring, data, Command centre, Control centre will be available, 24 x 7 x 365 for the units installed in the city. The unit is designed to reduce air pollution & the waste is collected and disposed of as per applicable laws for waste disposal which is non-hazardous in nature.
