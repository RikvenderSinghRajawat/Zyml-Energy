National Clean Air Programme (NCAP)

Government of India launched the National Clean Air Programme (NCAP) in 2019 to tackle the air pollution problem in a comprehensive manner, with a target to achieve 20-30% reduction in particulate matter (PM) concentrations by 2024.

The NCAP is implemented in 132 cities, with a focus on achieving Sustainable Development Goals 3, 11, and 13 which have direct reference to clean air and reducing air pollution.
